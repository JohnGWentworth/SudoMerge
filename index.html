How to Play

01

Swipe to move tiles. Identical tiles merge to the next number: 1+1=2, 2+2=3... up to 9.

02

Clear space by completing a set of 1, 2, 3, and 4 in any row or column. They will vanish and award 500 pts.

03

Efficiency Matters. The global leaderboard ranks by Score first, and Fewest Moves second. Every swipe counts!
